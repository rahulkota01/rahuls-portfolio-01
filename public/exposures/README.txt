HOW TO ADD YOUR IMAGES:

1. Copy your images into this folder (public/exposures/)

2. Name them according to the ID in portfolio-data.ts:
   - 1.jpg or 1.png → For "Healthcare Summit 2025 - IISc Bangalore"
   - 2.jpg or 2.png → For "GIAN Program - IIT Indore"
   - 3.jpg or 3.png → For "National E-Poster Competition"
   - 4.jpg or 4.png → For "Research Lab Visit - IIT BHU"
   - 5.jpg or 5.png → For "Conference Presentation - QISFEST"
   - 6.jpg or 6.png → For "Workshop on Computational Biology" (custom)
   - 7.jpg or 7.png → For "Your Next Research Exposure" (custom)
   - 8.jpg or 8.png → For "Your Research Exposure" (custom)

3. Recommended image specifications:
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 800x600, 1200x900)
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - File size: Under 500KB for optimal loading

4. Update portfolio-data.ts to point to your images:
   - Find the researchExposures array
   - Change: image: "data:image/svg+..."
   - To: image: "/exposures/1.jpg"
